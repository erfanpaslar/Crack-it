The rules are simple, you guess a number and program will give you a hint by ○ or ● depending on the digits you guessed is 'in' or 'in the right place'.

For example let's say the number is 12345, and you guess 54321. so all five digits you guessed is in number but none of them in the right place (○○○○○)

Or, if you guess 16354, just four digits are in the number and two of them in the right place (●●○○)

If you guess 98760, then none of them are either in the number or in the right place so nothing will show up

Simply every circle(●○) represents that a digit is in the number, and every filled circles(●) represents that a digit is in the right place.

Remember the hints said nothing about the order of digits you guessed. and multiple digits are not allowed.
